Meet the Secretariat

Elias Alstead is the Director of Technology for WAMUNC XX. He previously served as the Deputy Director of Technology for WAMUNC XIX. Elias is a sophomore majoring in computer science. Raised in Gig Harbor, Washington, Elias developed a love for the outdoors in the great Pacific Northwest. Since starting school at GW, he has gotten involved with several intramural sports. Outside of classes and activities, Elias enjoys hiking, playing basketball, and exploring DC with friends.
