Christabel Chan

Secretary General

secretarygeneral@wamunc.com

Christabel Chan is the Secretary General of WAMUNC XX. She has served on the WAMUNC Secretariat for the past two years as both Chief of Staff and the Director of Conference Relations. As a result, WAMUNC has become an integral part of her undergraduate career.

Christabel is a junior in the Columbian College of Arts and Sciences majoring in Biology, minoring in Applied Ethics, and is a student in the GW School of Medicine and Health Science’s 7-year BA/MD program. In addition to being a Model United Nations fanatic, Christabel is an avid reader, world traveller, and volunteer at Children’s National Medical Center. She’s thrilled to welcome you to Washington D.C. for the 20th anniversary of WAMUNC!

Sky Singer

Executive Director

execdir@wamunc.com

Sky Singer is the Executive Director for WAMUNC XX. She previously served as the Under Secretary General for International Bodies for WAMUNC XIX. Sky is a senior in the Columbian College of Arts and Science studying Political Science and Journalism and Mass Communication.

A born and raised New Yorker, Sky began her model UN career in her freshman year of high school. At GW, she writes for the school newspaper, the Hatchet, and is a member of the national honor fraternity, Phi Sigma Pi. Over the past couple of years, Sky has been interning at various broadcast news outlets including NBC and MSNBC. A huge foodie at heart, she can usually be found eating and trying new restaurants around DC in her free time. Sky is excited to welcome all the delegations to WAMUNC XX in the spring!

Austin Meo

Chief of Staff

staff@wamunc.com

Austin is Chief of Staff for WAMUNC XX. He previously served on Secretariat as the Under Secretary General of Regional Bodies and Councils for WAMUNC XIX. He’s a junior double majoring in Political Science and Criminal Justice in the Columbian College of Arts and Sciences and is originally from Princeton Junction, NJ.

Outside of class, Austin enjoys all things sports related- suffering through the stress of being a NY Mets, Jets, Rangers and Knicks fan. This past year Austin interned for US Senator Cory Booker on Capitol Hill. He also serves as the Executive Vice President of the GW Residence Hall Association and is a Disabled Students Volunteer. Austin is looking forward to another great WAMUNC!

Danielle Rosenthal

Senior Advisor

senioradvisor@wamunc.com

Danielle Rosenthal is the Senior Advisor for WAMUNC XX. She previously served as the Under Secretary General for Historic and Specialized Bodies for WAMUNC XIX, as well as the Director of Academic Programming for the George Washington University International Affairs Society.

Danielle is a junior in the Elliott School of International Affairs with majors in International Affairs and Economics. She also competes as a part of the GW traveling Model UN team and has interned on Capitol Hill with both the House of Representatives and Senate during her time at GW. A San Francisco native, Danielle can often be seen exploring the museums and sights of DC, studying at the Library of Congress, or watching hockey and basketball.

Kellie Bancalari

Robert Kang

Director of Security

security@wamunc.com

Robert Kang is the Director of Security for WAMUNC XX. Robert worked as an intern at the Department of State, supporting emergency management responsibilities of overseas posts. He currently works as an investigative support assistant in the Department of Homeland Security. He is also one of the supervisors for GW Police Department Student Access Monitor program.

Robert is a senior at GW majoring in Political Science and Criminal Justice. He has lived in five different cities in the past: Seoul, Minneapolis, Irvine, Rome, and Paris. Organized yet spontaneous at times, Robert loves traveling and eating, likes learning about different cultures, and enjoys being active. Previously serving as the Director of Safety and Security for WAMUNC XIX, Robert is excited for his second year at WAMUNC!

Anson Mersereau

USG for Crisis Committees

Anson Mersereau is the Under Secretary General for Crisis Committees for WAMUNC XX. He previously served as the Vice Head Delegate for GW’s Model UN Travel Team, where he specialized in Crisis Committees.
