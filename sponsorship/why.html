Why

Thank you very much for your interest in supporting our organization. WAMUNC is a non-profit organization and a subsidiary of the George Washington University, a registered 501c3. WAMUNC is dedicated to providing the most enjoyable educational experience for high school students from around the world. Model UN brings together ambitious, curious, and motivated students from all backgrounds to participate in an exercise that promotes the development of valuable skills. The experiences that delegates have at model UN conference are impactful, and they walk away with new perspectives and more confidence in themselves. The model UN community is unique, and many of our GW staffers, having participated in model UN as delegates, know the importance of running an elite conference. It is with the aid from sponsors, like you, that allows us to put on the best programming we possibly can for our participants.
